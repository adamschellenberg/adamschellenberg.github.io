About Adam

Adam is a pretty hard-working guy. He's got a wife, Jenifer, and two kids, Levi and Annabelle. He's been in love with programming for a long time now, and is always thinking up new ways he could use technology to better his and his family's life.
Adam has dabbled in a few different things within programming. C++ was his first time learning how to code, but he quickly made a switch to C# for game development. After playing around with that for a while, he discovered his passion: web development. And video games. And well, anything that involves coding. But mostly web development.
Outside of programming, Adam loves to be creative, whether it's painting, drawing, writing, baking, or asking his wife how to take a good photo.
