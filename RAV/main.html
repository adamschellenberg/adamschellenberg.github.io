Our Mission

  • Provide a stable and supportive work environment for those that have, or are currently, serving our country
  • Provide a stable environment for veterans and their families

Other resources to help them on their journey

Mission Statement

We started Rent-A-Vet Services in July 2016 due to our passion to help veterans within our community. Our passion grew into a vision of one day having a facility where we can provide housing and workplace training for our fellow veterans. We will create a safe place where they can be treated with the dignity they deserve and have the opportunities to build a better life for themselves and for their families. This is why and how Rent-A-Vet WGY6 was created. We are hoping to turn our Rent-A-Vet WGY6 vision into a reality in 2020!

Our goal for 2020-2021 is to purchase and establish a community living space where veterans will live, receive training in home improvement skills, and have access to equine therapy and other resources to help them along on their journey. We are looking to acquire a property with an existing main house, barn, and plenty of land where we can grow Rent-A-Vet WGY6 into a place with enough housing and facilities to help as many veterans as we can. With your help we can accomplish our vision one phase at a time!
